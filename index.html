<h1><center><u> About of Okoboji </h1></center></u>


<p><center>

In the state of Iowa there is a town on the eastern shore of West Okoboji Lake called Okoboji, located in Dickinson County. It is also called, The Iowa Great Lakes. Okoboji is a massive tourist area in the summer time. People from around the world come to see the beautiful glacier lakes and everything else Okoboji has to offer. Thanks to Spirit Lake and Arnolds Park contributing to Okoboji area there has been an increase in restaurants, stores, resorts etc. This caused Okoboji to become a recognizable name amongst Iowa and other states due to a variety of products being sold. </center></p>
